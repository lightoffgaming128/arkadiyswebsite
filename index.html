Meet Arkadiy

Built on care, not shortcuts.

My name is Arkadiy Smirnoff, and I started Arkadiy's Mobile Detailing out of a genuine passion for making vehicles look their absolute best. I'm 14 years old, a local student, and I've been running this business for over eight months — balancing school with a real commitment to quality.

I specialize in paint correction, ceramic coatings, and full detailing. For me, detailing isn't just about cleaning cars — it's about restoring them, protecting them, and making them stand out. I take my time with every vehicle because the results matter to me just as much as they do to you.

Outside of detailing, I'm an active triathlete and mountain biker. Those sports have taught me discipline, consistency, and attention to detail — the same qualities I bring to every job. I'm proud of my background as an immigrant from Russia with Russian and Ukrainian heritage, who moved to California in 2015. Starting this business is how I'm building something meaningful and working toward my future.

When you choose Arkadiy's Mobile Detailing, you're supporting someone who truly cares. I'm committed to exceeding your expectations — one detail at a time.
